About Us

Welcome to CAM Construction

At CAM Construction, we are committed to delivering high-quality construction services with integrity and excellence. Our company, named with the values of "Christ Above Man" in mind, reflects our dedication to honesty, trust, and superior craftsmanship in every project we undertake.

Our Founders

CAM Construction was founded by Charlie and Ashley McGuire, a dynamic husband-and-wife team with a passion for construction and a commitment to community. With years of experience in the industry, Charlie and Ashley have built CAM Construction on a foundation of strong ethics, superior skills, and unwavering dedication to client satisfaction.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build strong foundations, not just in the literal sense but also through strong client relationships and community ties. We believe in the power of quality construction to transform spaces and improve lives, and we strive to exceed expectations in every project we handle.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive Services: We provide a wide range of construction services, including residential, commercial, and industrial projects. From initial planning and design to final execution, we handle every aspect of the construction process.
  • Expert Team: Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering top-notch results. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to ensure the highest quality in every project.
  • Customer Focus: At CAM Construction, our clients are our top priority. We work closely with each client to understand their unique needs and deliver tailored solutions that meet and exceed their expectations.
  • Community Engagement: We are proud to contribute to our local community by supporting various initiatives and participating in community-building projects.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and integrity in all our actions.
  • Quality: We deliver exceptional quality in every project, paying attention to detail and striving for perfection.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our success is measured by the satisfaction of our clients. We are committed to ensuring that every client is happy with our work.
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and innovative techniques to enhance our services and deliver better results.

Get in Touch

We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our services and past projects. If you have any questions or would like to discuss your construction needs, please feel free to contact us. We look forward to working with you and turning your vision into reality.
